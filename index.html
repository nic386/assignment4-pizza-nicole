Diesel Cakes

Desserts are Better than the Main Course

These are desserts that I have created to satisfy one's sweet tooth, while also fueling one's body. These lovely treats are vegan, gluten free and packed with protein. They taste amazing, not like chalk as many gluten free desserts do. Even people who are not on a 'health kick' enjoy eating diesel cakes!

"There is always room for dessert!"
